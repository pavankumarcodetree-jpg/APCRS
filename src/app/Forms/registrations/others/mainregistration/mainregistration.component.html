Registrations

Birth Registration Form

How to register Birth Form

  • 1.Obtain the Birth Registration Form

    Visit your local birth registration office, municipal office, or health department.

  • 2.Fill Out the Registration Form

    Child’s name

    Gender

    Date and place of birth

    Parents' information (names, addresses, etc.)

  • 3. Submit Supporting Documents

    Proof of Birth

    Parental IDs

    Marriage Certificate (if applicable)

  • 4. Pay the Registration Fee

  • 5. Receive the Birth Certificate

Death Registration Form

How to register Death Form

  • 1. Obtain the Death Registration Form

    Visit your local birth registration office, municipal office, or health department.

  • 2.Fill Out the Registration Form

    Provide the details of the deceased, including name, age, gender, and cause of death.

    Mention the relationship of the informant to the deceased.

  • 3. Submit Supporting Documents

    Medical Certificate of Cause of Death

    Deceased’s ID proof

    Informant’s ID proof (if necessary)

  • 4. Pay the Registration Fee

  • 5. Receive the Death Certificate
